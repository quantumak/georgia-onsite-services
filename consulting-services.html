Are you a small to medium business, waste broker, or industrial service provider that just doesn’t have the need or budget to staff a full time waste manager? Would an “as needed” or short term professional be the answer that you’re looking for?



Georgia On-Site Services, LLC provides both short and long term comprehensive consulting and waste management solutions for companies in most industries and of all sizes. Allow us to listen to your needs and tailor an option for you.​​
  • On site compliance inspections of waste storage, satellite accumulation, and disposal areas
  • Evaluation of current waste disposal vendor costs and compliance history. Provide cost efficient alternatives that are both in good regulatory standing and are customer service oriented
  • Waste profile, shipping document and label preparation
  • Provide an experienced waste professional on shipment day to ensure waste containers are marked/labeled, loaded/segregated, and that all documentation is completed according to Federal, State and local regulatory requirements
